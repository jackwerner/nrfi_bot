Red Sox acquired Willson Contreras from Cardinals in a Sunday night deal. Veteran waived his no-trade clause to join Boston, just like Sonny Gray did last month. Contreras hit .257 with 20 homers and a 124 wRC+ in 2025 while playing first base.
Rays shipped Shane Baz to the Orioles for four prospects including 2025 first-rounders Slater de Brun and Caden Bodine. Tampa Bay continues their pattern of flipping established talent for future assets while Baltimore adds another arm for their playoff push.
Cubs sign reliever Jacob Webb to one-year deal with club option for 2027. $1.5M for 2026 with incentives that could push it to $2M. Classic Hoyer bullpen move after finally breaking his streak of one-year deals with Maton.
Marlins reliever Ronny Henriquez underwent UCL reconstruction surgery and will miss the entire 2026 season. Tough blow after his breakout 2025 campaign with 7 saves, 98 Ks and a 199 ERA+ across 69 appearances.
Mets shipped Jeff McNeil to the Athletics for teenage pitching prospect Yordan Rodriguez. McNeil played 48 games in the outfield last season, creating another vacancy as the Mets continue dismantling their core. Stearns clearly making room for top prospect Carson Benge.

Kenley Jansen heading to Detroit on a one-year deal pending physical. Tigers building a solid bullpen after re-signing Finnegan last week. Veteran closer sits just 24 saves shy of the 500 milestone.
Pete Fairbanks heading to Miami on a one-year, $13 million deal. One of the better bullpen arms in baseball with a 2.83 ERA and 59 Ks in 60 innings for the Rays last season.
