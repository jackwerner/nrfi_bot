Rob Refsnyder just signed a one-year, $6.25M deal with the Mariners. Solid veteran pickup that gives Seattle some outfield depth and a guy who can handle first base too.
Pete Fairbanks heading to Miami on a one-year, $13M deal with $1M in incentives. Solid pickup for the Marlins bullpen after he posted a 2.83 ERA with Tampa last season.
Shane Baz traded from Rays to Orioles for four top prospects including 2025 first-rounders Slater de Brun and Caden Bodine. Baltimore keeps loading up after already adding Alonso and Ward this winter.
Pirates landing Ryan O'Hearn on a two-year, $29M deal after his All-Star season. Gives them another solid bat to pair with Brandon Lowe in that revamped lineup they've been building all winter.
Jorge Polanco and the Mets just finalized a two-year, $40M deal. He's expected to play first base and DH, giving them some flexibility after losing Alonso to the Orioles. Solid bat coming off a career-high 134 OPS+ last season.
Tyler Rogers heading to Toronto on a three-year, $37M deal with a fourth-year vesting option. Blue Jays bullpen getting a major boost with the veteran reliever who should pair nicely with their revamped pitching staff.
