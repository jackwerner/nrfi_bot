Rockies picked up Jake McCarthy from the D-backs for pitching prospect Josh Grosz on Saturday. McCarthy brings speed and versatility after hitting .285 with 25 steals in 2024, while Grosz heads to his second organization in six months.

Braves brought back Tyler Kinley on a one-year, $4.25 million deal after declining his $5.5 million option in November. Smart financial move by Atlanta to retain the reliever at a lower price while still getting a club option for 2027.
Brewers ace Freddy Peralta generating serious trade interest from Yankees, Mets, Dodgers, Braves and Red Sox. $8 million salary for 2026 makes him incredibly valuable compared to what similar starters cost in free agency.
