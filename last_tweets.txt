Merrill Kelly heading back to Arizona on a two-year, $40 million deal after brief stint with Rangers. Solid veteran arm returns to familiar territory where he's had most of his MLB success.
Braves making big bullpen moves this week, adding Robert Suarez on a three-year deal after signing Yastrzemski to a two-year contract. Suarez posted a 2.97 ERA with 40 saves in 2025 and will set up for Iglesias. Atlanta might not be done yet with Gallen still on their radar.
Yankees reportedly in serious talks with Brewers for Freddy Peralta. Multiple teams including Red Sox, Giants, Orioles and Astros also showing interest in the starter who posted a strong 2025 season. Brewers asking price remains high.
Former Rangers outfielder Adolis García finalizing a $10M deal with the Phillies. Looking for a rebound season in the middle of their lineup after Castellanos departure. That's a solid one-year gamble for a playoff contender.
Braves lock down their shortstop position by bringing back Ha-Seong Kim on a one-year, $20M deal. Smart move to retain the September waiver claim who provides solid defense up the middle.
D-backs still looking to move Ketel Marte despite recent pitching signings, while Phillies finalize one-year, $10M deal with Adolis García to replace Castellanos in right field. García gives Philly a power bat with defensive upside.
Reliever Chris Martin heading back to Texas on a one-year deal. Becomes the first bullpen arm Rangers have brought back from their 2025 pen that struggled down the stretch.
Rays finalize $15M deal with Steven Matz, giving him shot to return to rotation despite bullpen success last year. Neander: "There's a real desire on his end to want to start again" and they're letting him prep as a starter this spring.
