Marlins drawing serious interest for Sandy Alcantara after his strong finish to 2025. The former Cy Young winner posted a 2.68 ERA in his final eight starts after struggling in his return from Tommy John surgery.
Yankees and Marlins discussing potential reunion with Devin Williams after his rollercoaster 2025 season. His 4.79 ERA doesn't tell the whole story - still struck out plenty of batters despite some bad luck.
Angels betting on Grayson Rodriguez's upside after sending Taylor Ward to Baltimore. Former top prospect missed all of 2025 with injuries but could be the rotation piece LA desperately needs if he returns to form.
Brandon Woodruff accepting his $21M player option with Milwaukee after missing most of 2024-25. Only the second Brewer ever making $20M+ annually alongside Yelich. Great news for their rotation if he stays healthy.
Dylan Cease signs with Blue Jays for seven years, $210 million. Top arm in free agency brings durability with five straight seasons of 32+ starts and elite stuff to bolster Toronto's rotation after their World Series run.
Mets and Rangers pulled off the first blockbuster of the offseason with Marcus Semien heading to New York and Brandon Nimmo to Texas. Creates an outfield hole for the Mets that could make them players for Kyle Tucker on the market.
Ryan Helsley and the Orioles agree on a two-year deal with an opt-out after the first season. Baltimore continues building their bullpen after trading for Devin Williams earlier this offseason.
Rays prospect Xavier Isaac revealed he underwent "life saving" brain surgery in July after what was initially thought to be dehydration. The former first-rounder was limited to just 41 Double-A games in 2025 before the unexpected medical emergency.
